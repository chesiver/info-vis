Speed Dating Visualization


The data we are visualizing were originally collected as part of a 2002-2004 study at Columbia University. You can find the paper here and the data here.

Our visualization shows data from 227 men and 225 women across 17 different speed dating events, for a total of ~7,000 dates.

As part of the dates, each person evaluated their partner by distributing 100 points across 5 attributes:
- Attractiveness
- Sincerity
- Intelligence
- Fun
- Ambitiousness
